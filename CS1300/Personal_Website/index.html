Introduction

Hello, my name is Torin. I am excited to share a little bit about myself and my journey to Atlas.

Educational Background

My educational journey has been shaped by overcoming challenges associated with learning disabilities such as ADHD, dyslexia, and autism. These conditions have presented unique hurdles, requiring me to develop innovative strategies to succeed academically. Despite these challenges, I have remained dedicated to my education, driven by a desire to learn new and exciting skills.
Throughout my time in early education, I had to employ various techniques to manage my disabilities. For instance, I used specialized learning tools and sought additional support from teachers and mentors. My journey has been one of persistence, adaptation, and resilience, and I have learned to leverage my unique strengths to achieve my goals.

Why I Chose This Field of Study

My interest in tech began when I would explore web games at the family computer on the weekends. When I was in junior high I spent a few weeks at a robotics summercamp. I recall enjoying the process and payoff of programming. I was drawn to this field because I love the activity of programming and I believe this field with enable life of oppurtunity for me. I am particularly interested in developing applications that would be run by a user directly because I find that having unique, engaging, and accessible tools is empowering to users across the web.

Why I Chose This School

Choosing Atlas was a pivotal decision in my academic career. I was impressed by the unique study structure and the close bond students of all stages have with each other. The commitment to adaptive teaching and accessibility aligns perfectly with my personal and academic goals. I believe that Atlas offers an environment where I can thrive and make meaningful contributions.

My specialization and Why I Chose It

I decided to pursue the Full Stack specialization, or spec, because my interest in programming is specifically tailored to user interactions and stylish web design. This spec allows me to learn specific skills that are directly applicable to projects that align with my interests.

Conclusion

In conclusion, my journey in Programming has been deeply fulfilling, and I am eager to continue growing and learning at Atlas. I look forward to making the most of the opportunities available and contributing to the community.
